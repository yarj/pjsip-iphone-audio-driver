Audio units provide the mechanism for applications to achieve low-latency audio input and output. They can generate, process, receive, or otherwise manipulate streams of audio in near-real-time.

iPhone OS ships with audio units that support mixing, equalization, format conversion, and I/O for recording, playback, or live chat.
